Name Changes

King's Affair 2024

  • Next

Step 1

Log in to the ticketing system and click on your existing booking to view the details. You may need to log in using your Cambridge Raven account. Alternatively, you can use the link in your confirmation email.

View full size

Step 2

Click on the "Change the name on a ticket" option under the "Change or update your booking" section.

View full size

Step 3

You will be given the option to select the ticket you wish to change and enter the new name. Click "Next" to pay the name change fee and complete the process.

Please note that once you have clicked "Next", you will not be able to change the name again. If you have entered the wrong name or wish to revert the name change, get in touch with us.

View full size

Step 4

Upon successful completion of the name change, you will receive an email with the updated ticket details. This email can then be forwarded to the new ticketholder.

Alternatively, upon accessing your ticket through the link in the email, you can click the "Share with a friend" button below the QR code and share the generated link with the new ticketholder. This will allow them to add the ticket to their own OXEX account.
